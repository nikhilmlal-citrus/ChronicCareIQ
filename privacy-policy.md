# Privacy Policy for Chronic Care IQ Internal Integrations

Last Updated: April 22, 2026

At Chronic Care IQ, we value your privacy and the security of our corporate data. This Privacy Policy describes how the Chronic Care IQ n8n-QuickBooks Integration ("Integration") collects, uses, and protects data.

## 1. Data Collection
The Integration acts as a bridge between our internal systems and our QuickBooks Online account. We collect the following types of data:

Authentication Data: OAuth2 tokens required to connect to QuickBooks Online.

Transactional Data: Financial, invoice, and customer data pulled from QuickBooks Online to be synced with our internal databases.

Logs: Technical logs used to monitor the health and performance of the Integration.

## 2. How We Use Data
The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:
- Automating financial data synchronization.
- Ensuring operational efficiency and accurate reporting within our internal systems.
- Troubleshooting technical issues.

## 3. Data Security and HIPAA Compliance
Because we operate in the healthcare sector, data security is paramount.

- Access Control: Access to the Integration is restricted to authorized personnel only.
- Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using industry-standard TLS protocols.
- Data Residency: Data processed by the Integration remains within our controlled infrastructure. We do not sell or share any user or business data with unauthorized third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The Integration uses Intuit (QuickBooks Online) services. By using this Integration, you acknowledge that your data is shared with Intuit to facilitate synchronization. We ensure that our connection with Intuit complies with their developer terms and our own strict internal data privacy policies.

## 5. Retention
Data processed by the Integration is retained only for as long as necessary to perform business functions or as required by our internal data retention and compliance policies.

## 6. Changes to this Policy
Chronic Care IQ may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Integration constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms.

## Contact Us
For questions regarding this policy or to report a security concern, please contact info@chroniccareiq.com.
